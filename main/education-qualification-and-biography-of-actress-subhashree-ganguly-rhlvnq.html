Subhashree Ganguly was born in Kolkata, India and attended the School of Creative and Performing Arts.
She completed her schooling from the South Point High School, Kolkata.
 She graduated from the St. Xavier's College, Kolkata with a degree in Chemistry.
She made her acting debut in the Bengali-language film Rangbaaz in 2013.
She has also starred in films such as Romeo vs Juliet, Herogiri, and Badshah.
She has won several awards for her performances, including the Zee Bangla Gourab Award for Best Actress, the Kalakar Award for Best Supporting Actress, and the Star Jalsha Parivaar Award for Best Actress.
She has also featured in several ad campaigns for brands such as Britannia, Tanishq, and Tata Sky.
