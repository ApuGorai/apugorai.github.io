Get to Know Bollywood Actress Disha Patani: Wiki, Height, Age, Boyfriend, Family, Biography & More
Disha Patani is an Indian actress and model who has worked in Hindi, Telugu, and Kannada films.
She was born on June 13, 1992 in Bareilly, Uttar Pradesh.
She is best known for her roles in MS Dhoni: The Untold Story (2016), Kung Fu Yoga (2017), and Bharat (2019).
She has also appeared in several music videos, such as Befikra, Humma Humma, and Slow Motion.
Disha stands at a height of 5'7" and weighs around 54 kg.
Disha has won several awards, including the Stardust Award and the Zee Cine Award.
She is active on social media platforms such as Instagram, Facebook, and Twitter.
