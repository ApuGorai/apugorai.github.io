Sonampreet Bajwa (born 16 August 1989) is an Indian model and actress who predominantly appears in Punjabi language films [1], alongside few Hindi films.
She has appeared in films such as Sardaarji 2 (2016), Nikka Zaildar (2016) and Ardaas Karaan (2019).
She began her career as a model, before making her debut in the Punjabi film industry with the film Best of Luck (2013).

She has also appeared in a few Hindi films, including Airlift (2016).
She has won the PTC Punjabi Film Award for Best Actress for her performance in the film Nikka Zaildar.
She is a social media influencer, with over 4.5 million followers on Instagram.
She has also appeared on several television shows, including Dil Diyan Gallan, where she hosts the show alongside other celebrities.
She is a graduate of Sri Bhagawan Mahaveer Jain College in Bengaluru, India, where she studied Mechanical Engineering.
