Janhvi Kapoor is an Indian actress who made her debut in 2018 with the film Dhadak.
She was born on 6 March 1997 in Mumbai, India.
She completed her schooling from Dhirubhai Ambani International School in Mumbai.
She later joined Lee Strasberg Theatre and Film Institute in Los Angeles to pursue acting.
She made her film debut with the movie Dhadak alongside Ishaan Khattar, which was a remake of the Marathi film Sairat.
She was later seen in the 2019 movie Ghost Stories, and has several upcoming projects.
She has received awards and nominations for her performance in the film Dhadak, including a nomination for the Filmfare Award for Best Female Debut.
