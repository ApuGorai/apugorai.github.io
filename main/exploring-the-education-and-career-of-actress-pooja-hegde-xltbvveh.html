Pooja Hegde was born in Mumbai, India and attended the International Curriculum School in her hometown.
She graduated as a Computer Engineering major from the KC College of Engineering, Mumbai.
She made her acting debut in 2010 with the Telugu film Mugamoodi.
She received her first nomination for the Filmfare Award for Best Supporting Actress for her performance in the 2016 Telugu romantic comedy Oka Laila Kosam.
She was part of the ensemble cast of the 2017 Telugu action comedy Duvvada Jagannadham, for which she received a nomination for the Filmfare Award for Best Actress - Telugu.
She starred in the 2019 Telugu-language action drama Maharshi, opposite N. T. Rama Rao Jr.
She has featured in several ad campaigns and has endorsed brands such as Parachute Advansed, Kalyan Jewellers, and Manappuram Gold Loan.
